Codebases become risky when structure is invisible.

Most engineering delays are not from writing code; they are from uncertainty about impact. Manual traversal across files and commits hides dependency pathways that decide whether a change is safe.

Without architecture visibility, reviews rely on intuition, timeline context gets fragmented, and blast radius is guessed too late.

Built for engineers who need structural clarity, not abstract AI text.
